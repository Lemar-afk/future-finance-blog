Top 3 AI Tools for Personal Finance

Published: May 2025

Managing your finances has never been easier — or smarter. With the rise of artificial intelligence, you can now automate savings, track spending, and plan budgets using AI-powered tools. Here are the top 3 AI tools revolutionizing personal finance:

1. Cleo

Cleo is a chatbot-based AI financial assistant that connects to your bank and gives you budgeting advice with a playful tone. It helps analyze your spending and even roasts you when you're overspending.

2. YNAB (You Need A Budget)

YNAB is a proactive budgeting app that encourages you to “give every dollar a job.” Their AI-powered trends and insights show you where you’re overspending and how to hit savings goals faster.

3. ChatGPT

Using ChatGPT, you can get customized budgeting tips, savings strategies, and investment ideas tailored to your income, expenses, and lifestyle. It’s like having a financial advisor on-demand.

Final Thoughts: Embracing AI can take your money game to the next level. Try integrating one (or all) of these tools to save smarter and stress less.
